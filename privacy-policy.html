1. Introduction

Welcome to iPerspective. I'm committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using this application. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the mobile application and related services.

By using iPerspective, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, I collect information that can be used to identify you:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture
  • Age or date of birth
  • Gender
  • Location (city, state, country)
  • Work industry
  • Relationship status

User Content

I collect the content you create when using iPerspective:

  • Photos and images you share
  • Questions you create and answers you provide
  • Comments and messages
  • Your interests and preferences

Usage Information

I automatically collect certain information about how you interact with the app:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • How and when you use the app
  • Community interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve iPerspective
  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with compatible communities based on your interests, opinions, and location
  • Enable community interactions and communications
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send you updates and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

When you join communities, other members will see your profile information, content you share, and responses you provide. This is how connections are made.

With Service Providers

I may share your information with third-party vendors who help operate the app, such as data storage and analytics services.

For Legal Reasons

I may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

With Your Consent

I may share your information with third parties when you have given consent to do so.

5. Data Security

I implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

While I strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, I cannot guarantee absolute security. You use iPerspective at your own discretion.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your information:

  • Account Information: Review and update your info anytime in settings
  • Communication Preferences: Opt out of promotional emails
  • Privacy Settings: Control what's visible to other users
  • Delete Your Account: Delete your account anytime through app settings

Depending on your location, you may also have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Rectify or update your information
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

7. Children's Privacy

iPerspective is not directed to children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If I learn that I have collected information from a child under 13, I will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When I do, I'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify you through the app. Your continued use of iPerspective after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
