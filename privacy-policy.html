Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

Perspective is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application, Perspective.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Information such as your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Location Data: Used to create local communities. You can disable this in your settings.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app to improve its performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience.
  • Create and manage communities.
  • Communicate updates and notifications to you.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared with:

  • Service providers to facilitate app functionality.
  • Authorities if required by law.

4. Data Security

While we implement measures to protect your data, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Your Data Rights

You can contact us to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.

6. Third-Party Links

Our app may link to third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
