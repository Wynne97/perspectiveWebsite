About Perspective

Our mission is to create meaningful connections in a digital world by bringing people together through shared perspectives, interests, and locations.

Our Story

How Perspective came to be

Perspective was born from a simple observation: despite being more connected than ever through technology, many people feel increasingly isolated and disconnected from meaningful relationships.

Our founder, Shane, noticed that traditional social media platforms were creating shallow connections based on content consumption rather than genuine interaction. He envisioned a different approachâ€”one that would use technology to facilitate meaningful relationships based on shared perspectives and interests.

In 2024, Shane assembled a team of developers, designers, and community builders who shared his vision. Together, they created Perspective, a social platform designed to connect people in small, intimate communities where genuine conversation and connection could flourish.

Today, Perspective is growing into a vibrant ecosystem of micro-communities, bringing together like-minded individuals who might never have met otherwise. We're proud to be building technology that serves human connection rather than replacing it.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Meaningful Connection

We believe in quality over quantity. Small communities foster deeper relationships and more meaningful interactions.

Authenticity

We encourage genuine self-expression and honest communication. Real connections can only form when people show up as their authentic selves.

Privacy & Safety

We prioritize user privacy and create safe spaces for interaction. Trust is the foundation of any meaningful relationship.

Community

We believe in the power of community to enrich lives. Our platform is designed to bring people together both online and in the real world.

Inclusivity

We welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds. Different viewpoints enrich conversation and broaden understanding.

Sustainability

We're building for the long term. Our business model aligns with our mission to create value for users and society.

Our Impact

How Perspective is changing social connection

Combating Loneliness

In a world where loneliness has become an epidemic, Perspective creates meaningful connections that help people feel seen, heard, and understood.

Strengthening Local Communities

By connecting people based on location, Perspective helps strengthen local communities and encourages real-world interaction.

Fostering Understanding

Through shared perspectives and open dialogue, Perspective helps bridge divides and foster understanding between different viewpoints.
