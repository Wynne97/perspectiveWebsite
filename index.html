Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Perspective

How does Perspective match me with communities?

Perspective uses a sophisticated algorithm that considers your interests, opinions, location, and other factors to match you with like-minded individuals. Every three days, new communities are created to introduce you to fresh perspectives while maintaining compatibility.

What makes Perspective different from other social apps?

Unlike traditional social media that focuses on content consumption and large networks, Perspective creates intimate communities of up to 10 people who share similar worldviews. We prioritize meaningful conversation and real-world connections over passive scrolling.

How do I create or join a community?

When you sign up, you'll be automatically matched with communities based on your interests and location. You can also create your own communities around specific interests, and our algorithm will help find compatible members to join.

How do in-person meetups work?

Once you've connected with your community online, you can propose in-person meetups through the app. All members can suggest locations and times, and the app helps coordinate the details. Safety features are built in to ensure comfortable and secure meetings.
