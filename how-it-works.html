How It Works

Perspective is designed to foster meaningful connections by matching people with similar perspectives. Every three days, new communities are created to introduce you to fresh faces and shared ideas.

Step 1: Sign Up

Start by creating an account and selecting your interests. These interests are used to group you with like-minded individuals.

Step 2: Answer Questions

Engage in fun and thought-provoking questions that help define your unique perspective. These answers shape how you're matched with others.

Step 3: Join Communities

Be part of small communities of up to 10 people. Discuss topics, share opinions, and connect over common interests and perspectives.

What Makes Perspective Special?

Our platform focuses on teaching users how to control their digital data while fostering meaningful relationships. By prioritizing thoughtful interactions, we aim to counteract the effects of social media on social skills and cognitive connections.
