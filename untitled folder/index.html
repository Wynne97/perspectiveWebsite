We scroll through feeds full of strangers. We chase followers who don't really know us. We mistake likes for genuine connection. Meanwhile, the real conversations—the ones where someone actually gets you—feel harder to find than ever.

Find Your People

Small communities mean everyone gets heard. Discuss topics that matter. Discover where you agree—and where you see things differently.

Unite Around What Matters

Answer questions about yourself and guess how others would answer. iPerspective shows you exactly where your views overlap—and where they differ.

Every 3 days, communities refresh. You get matched with new people who share your perspectives. It keeps things fresh—and ensures you're always expanding your circle.

Made a real connection? Add them as a friend. Those relationships stay, even as communities evolve.
