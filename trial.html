What is iPerspective?

iPerspective is a social app where you join communities and answer polls about topics that actually matter to you — from values and relationships to career and culture.

Based on your answers, you get matched into small affinity groups with people who share your perspective. These aren't random strangers — they're people who actually think like you.

Once you're in a group, you play games, have real conversations, and build genuine connections. It's the opposite of doomscrolling. It's finding your people.
