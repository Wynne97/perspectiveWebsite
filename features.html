Powerful Features for Meaningful Connections

Discover how Perspective helps you build genuine relationships through shared interests, opinions, and locations.

Core Features

The building blocks of the Perspective experience

Micro-Communities

Join intimate groups of up to 10 people who share your interests, opinions, and worldview. These small communities foster meaningful conversation and genuine connection.

Perspective Matching

Our unique algorithm connects you with people who share your worldview, interests, and values. Every three days, new communities are formed based on compatibility.

Local Connections

Turn digital relationships into real-world friendships with people in your area. Find communities based on your city, state, or region.

Interactive Questions

Engage with thought-provoking questions about photos, topics, and community members. Share your perspective and learn from others.

Community Discussions

Participate in focused conversations within your communities. Share ideas, opinions, and experiences in a respectful environment.

Community Events

Organize and attend in-person meetups with your community members. Turn online connections into real-world relationships.

Community Types

Different ways to connect with like-minded individuals

Interest-Based

Connect with people who share your passions, hobbies, and interests. Whether you're into photography, hiking, cooking, or gaming, find your tribe.

Local

Meet people in your neighborhood, city, or region who share your perspective. Build a local network of like-minded individuals.

Professional

Network with professionals in your industry who share similar career goals. Exchange ideas, collaborate, and grow together.

Organization

Join communities within your organization. Connect with colleagues who share your interests and perspectives.

Question Types

Engaging ways to share perspectives and connect

Photo Questions

Share photos and ask specific questions about them. Get perspectives from your community members on your images.

Demographic Questions

Learn about the backgrounds and basic information of your community members through thoughtful demographic questions.

Personality Questions

Discover the personality traits and characteristics of your community members through engaging personality questions.

Lifestyle Questions

Share and learn about daily habits, routines, and lifestyle choices of your community members.

Social Questions

Explore social dynamics, preferences, and interaction styles through questions about social situations and relationships.

Interest-Based Questions

Dive deep into specific interests and passions through targeted questions about hobbies, entertainment, and activities.

Community Events

Taking online connections to the real world

1

Propose an Event

Any community member can suggest an in-person meetup. Choose a type of event, suggest a location, and propose dates and times.

2

Community Voting

Community members vote on the proposed event details. The app helps coordinate the best time and place based on everyone's preferences.

3

Event Confirmation

Once details are finalized, the event is confirmed. All community members receive notifications and can RSVP to the meetup.

4

Meet In Person

Connect with your community members face-to-face. The app provides safety features and check-ins to ensure a comfortable experience.
